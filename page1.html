About Artificial Intelligence

Discover what AI is, how it works, and its key components.

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It enables computers to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

Key Components of AI

  • Machine Learning: Computers learn from data without explicit programming.
  • Neural Networks: Algorithms inspired by the human brain.
  • Natural Language Processing: AI systems that interpret and generate human language.

How AI Works

AI relies on data, algorithms, and computational power. Models are trained on vast datasets to recognize patterns and make predictions, forming the backbone of modern AI technologies.
